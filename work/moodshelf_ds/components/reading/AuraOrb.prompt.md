A glowing orb that visualizes a reader's emotional mix — their "reading aura". Blends emotion colors into a living gradient. Use in recaps, profiles and share cards.

```jsx
<AuraOrb
  colors={['var(--emo-longing)', 'var(--emo-wonder)', 'var(--emo-joy)']}
  label="Longing"
  caption="Your June aura"
/>
```
