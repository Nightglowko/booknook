Round icon button for app bars, card corners and toolbars. The `glass` variant blurs over book imagery.

```jsx
<IconButton name="bookmark" variant="soft" label="Save" />
<IconButton name="heart" active onClick={toggleLike} />
<IconButton name="more-horizontal" variant="glass" />
```

Variants: `ghost` `soft` `solid` `glass`. `active` highlights and fills the heart glyph.
